NNBack

Simple Dual N-Back Task. By Peter Ellis Jones.

Instructions

Press enter to start. You will be presented with a sound and a square on the 3x3 grid to the left. Remember the sounds and square and press UP to get the next pattern. The goal of dual n-back is to remember the pattern that occured n-steps ago. If n is 2, then press LEFT if you hear a sound repeat after 2 steps, and RIGHT if you see a square repeat after 2 steps, or DOWN if both repeat after 2 steps. Good luck!
